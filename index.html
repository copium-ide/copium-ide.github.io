Welcome to a new era.

Copium IDE is a revolutionary, block-based editor designed for the modern creator. Inspired by the simplicity of Scratch, it empowers you to build games, applications, and even complex headless programs with an intuitive drag-and-drop interface. Forget frustrating syntax and boilerplate code; Copium IDE lets you focus purely on your logic and bring your ideas to life.

The Core: A Powerful Block Editor

The heart of Copium IDE is its flexible and robust block-based editor. We've refined the concept of visual programming to create a streamlined and efficient development environment.

  • Intuitive Block System: Construct complex programs by connecting logical blocks. This visual approach minimizes syntax errors and makes understanding program flow effortless.
  • Seamless Transition to Code: For those who want to look under the hood, Copium IDE can instantly display the text-based code equivalent of your block creations, providing a powerful learning and debugging tool.
